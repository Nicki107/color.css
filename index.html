Candied Yams

Made with love by Nikia Rivers

  • 6 cleaned sweet potatoes
  • 2 table spoons of cinnamon
  • 1 table spoon of nutmeg
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 table spoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of maple syrup
  • 2 sticks of unsalted butter
  • 1 cup of granola
  • 1 cup of mini marshmellows
  1. Wash sweet potatoes.
  2. Boil sweet potatoes for 25 minutes.
  3. Let potatoes cool for 15 minutes.
  4. Once cool dice, dice potatoes into cubes.
  5. Place cubes into a mixing bowl, mash potatoes into a mush consistentcy.
  6. Melt butter in a seperate bowl, combine cinnamon, brown sugar, nutmeg, vanilla extract and syrup together.
  7. Combine mixture to the potatoes.
  8. Place into a baking pan, put in the oven on 425 degrees Farenheit for 25 minutes.
  9. Take the yams out of the oven, then add the granola and marshmellows on top of the yams, place pan back in the oven for 10 minutes.
  10. Take out of oven and let the yams cool 10 minutes,then serve.

Candied yams is one of my favorite homecooked meals. It reminds me of a cool winter night with a warm hardy meal. The flavors of the candied yams fresh out of the oven are delicious. As a child, I watched my grandmother in the kitcken making this same receipe and taste tasting for her. Now I carry on the tradition with my family.
